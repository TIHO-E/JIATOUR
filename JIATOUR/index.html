About Us

Jia Tour is a Melbourne-based bus charter expert, providing transportation services across Victoria and Australia.

Our drivers have over 10 years of professional experience. Safety is always our top priority.

We are an Accredited Bus Operator (AO134410) certified by Transport Safety Victoria.

Our operations comply fully with the Bus Safety Act 2009 (VIC) and Bus Safety Regulations 2010 (VIC).

We specialise in:

  • Educational institutions
  • Corporate events
  • Travel agents
  • Family, wedding, club & private events
  • Wine tours
  • Snow trips
